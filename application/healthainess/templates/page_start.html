Start

The HealthAInes device will help you to exercise more, to exercise right and to inform your doctors about your progress.
Just tell the device what to do, it understands voice commands. "Start" will show this information, "Exercise" starts the exercises, "Report" shows the report.
Easy enough, isn't it?
During the exercises you can start and stop exercise by voice. Just say "Start" followed by the name of the exercise. You can end an exercise just by saying "Stop".
